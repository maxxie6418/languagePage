长难句分析

句子成分

一、主语（Subject）

主语是句子叙述的主体，表示句子描述的是"谁"或"什么"。可由名词、代词、数词、不定式、动名词、从句等充当。

例句

The student works hard. （名词作主语）

To learn English is important. （不定式作主语）

二、谓语（Predicate）

谓语说明主语"做什么"、"是什么"或"怎么样"。由动词或动词短语充当。

例句

She speaks English fluently.

They have been working on this project.

三、宾语（Object）

宾语表示动作的对象或承受者，位于及物动词或介词之后。

例句

I love music. （名词作宾语）

He gave me a book. （双宾语）

时态与语态

一、一般现在时

表示经常性、习惯性的动作或客观事实、真理。

结构：主语 + 动词原形/第三人称单数

The earth revolves around the sun.

二、现在完成时

表示过去发生的动作对现在造成的影响或结果。

结构：have/has + 过去分词

I have finished my homework.

三、被动语态

强调动作的承受者而非执行者。

结构：be + 过去分词

The window was broken by the boy.

从句

一、定语从句

修饰名词或代词的从句，由关系代词（who, whom, whose, which, that）或关系副词（where, when, why）引导。

例句

The man who is standing there is my teacher.

This is the house where I lived ten years ago.

二、名词性从句

包括主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句、同位语从句。

What he said is true. （主语从句）

I believe that he will come. （宾语从句）

三、状语从句

包括时间、地点、原因、目的、结果、条件、让步、方式、比较等九种。

If it rains tomorrow, we will stay at home. （条件状语从句）

Although he is young, he knows a lot. （让步状语从句）

非谓语动词

一、不定式（Infinitive）

to + 动词原形，可作主语、宾语、表语、定语、状语、补语。

To see is to believe. （主语、表语）

I want to go home. （宾语）

二、动名词（Gerund）

动词-ing形式，具有名词性质。

Swimming is good exercise. （主语）

He is fond of reading. （介词宾语）

三、分词（Participle）

现在分词（-ing）和过去分词（-ed），可作定语、表语、状语、补语。

a sleeping baby （现在分词作定语）

Seen from the hill, the city is beautiful. （过去分词作状语）

虚拟语气

一、与现在事实相反

从句：if + 主语 + 过去式（be动词用were）

主句：主语 + would/should/could/might + 动词原形

If I were you, I would accept the offer.

二、与过去事实相反

从句：if + 主语 + had + 过去分词

主句：主语 + would/should/could/might + have + 过去分词

If you had come earlier, you would have met him.

三、含蓄虚拟条件句

用 without, but for, otherwise, or 等代替 if 条件句。

Without your help, I would have failed.

倒装与强调

一、完全倒装

谓语动词全部置于主语之前。常见于表示方位、时间的副词或介词短语置于句首。

Here comes the bus.

On the wall hangs a painting.

二、部分倒装

只将助动词、情态动词或be动词置于主语之前。

Never have I seen such a beautiful sight.

Not only did he learn English, but he also learned French.

三、强调句型

It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 其他

It was Tom who/that broke the window.

长难句分析

分析步骤

Step 1：找谓语动词，确定句子主干

Step 2：找连接词，划分从句边界

Step 3：识别修饰成分（定语、状语、同位语）

Step 4：整合理解，翻译验证

真题例句分析

The emphasis given by both scholars and statesmen to the presumed disappearance of the American frontier helped to hide the critical importance of changes in the conditions and consequences of international trade that occurred during the second half of the nineteenth century.

结构拆解

主语：The emphasis

后置定语：given by both scholars and statesmen to the presumed disappearance of the American frontier

谓语：helped to hide

宾语：the critical importance

后置定语：of changes in the conditions and consequences of international trade

定语从句：that occurred during the second half of the nineteenth century

主旨大意题解题方法

一、题型特征

常见问法：What is the main idea of the passage? / The passage is mainly about ___ / What is the best title for the passage?

二、解题技巧

1. 首段+尾段法

考研英语阅读文章通常采用"总-分-总"或"总-分"结构，首段和尾段往往包含文章主旨。

2. 段落主题句叠加

将各段的主题句（通常是首句）串联起来，提炼出全文主旨。

3. 高频词法

关注文章中反复出现的关键词，它们往往指向文章主题。

三、干扰项特征

❌ 以偏概全：只涉及文章某一部分内容

❌ 过度推断：超出文章所述范围

❌ 无中生有：文章未提及的内容

❌ 正反混淆：与作者观点相反

四、真题演练

【2018 Text 1】What is the main idea of the passage?

A. The decline of traditional journalism

B. The rise of digital media platforms

C. The challenges facing American newspapers

【答案】C

解析：文章围绕美国报纸业面临的挑战展开，A、B均为细节，D未提及。

细节理解题解题方法

一、题型特征

常见问法：According to the passage, ___ / What do we learn about ___ from the passage?

二、解题步骤

Step 1：定位关键词

根据题干中的专有名词、数字、时间等关键词快速定位到原文。

Step 2：精读定位句

仔细阅读定位句及其前后句，理解具体细节。

Step 3：同义替换匹配

正确选项往往是原文的同义改写，注意同义词、近义词替换。

推理判断题解题方法

一、题型特征

常见问法：It can be inferred from the passage that ___ / What does the author imply about ___?

二、解题原则

1. 有据可循

推理必须基于原文信息，不能凭主观臆断。

2. 适度推理

只能推一步，不能过度推断。

3. 排除绝对

含有绝对词（must, never, always, all）的选项通常不是正确答案。

词义猜测题解题方法

一、解题方法

1. 上下文语境法

利用生词前后的解释、举例、同位语、定语从句等线索推断词义。

2. 逻辑关系法

利用转折（but, however）、因果（because, so）、并列（and, or）等逻辑关系推断。

3. 构词法

利用词根词缀知识拆分单词，推断大致含义。

态度观点题解题方法

完形填空 · 2018年Text1

The idea that some groups of people may be more intelligent than others is one of those hypotheses that dare not speak its name out loud. But the study of race and IQ has been a perennial battleground for opponents and proponents alike. In recent years, the debate has become more acrimonious, fueled by a controversial new book titled "The Bell Curve". The book argues that intelligence is largely inherited and that there are significant IQ differences between racial groups.

1. The word "perennial" in paragraph 1 is closest in meaning to ______.
A
permanent and continuing for a long time
B
violent and aggressive
C
popular and widely accepted
D
ancient and outdated

正确答案：A

解析：perennial 意为"多年生的；持续存在的"。根据上下文 "a perennial battleground for opponents and proponents alike"，可知这是支持者和反对者之间长期争论的领域。

重点词汇：perennial (adj.) 长期的；acrimonious (adj.) 激烈的；hypothesis (n.) 假设

完形填空 · 2019年Text2

The rise of "pseudo-science" has become a serious concern in the age of information. Many people now subscribe to theories that lack any scientific basis, from flat Earth beliefs to anti-vaccination movements. This trend poses a significant challenge to public health officials who must combat misinformation while respecting individual freedoms.

2. The phrase "subscribe to" in the passage is closest in meaning to ______.
A
believe in or support
B
pay money to receive
C
write down briefly
D
cancel or reject

正确答案：A

解析：subscribe to 在此语境下意为"认同，支持"，指的是人们认同某些缺乏科学依据的理论。

重点词汇：pseudo-science (n.) 伪科学；misinformation (n.) 虚假信息；vaccination (n.) 疫苗接种

阅读理解 · 2017年Text4

The French railway system is the product of a century of central planning. It has long been feared that robot displacement of humans would be to increased. Yet the evidence is for any productivity gains caused by computers is at best ambiguous. Even the European Parliament, which backed calls in February for stricter robot rules, acknowledged that "the development of robotics and artificial intelligence poses no additional risks to the safety and livelihood of European citizens beyond the risks posed by existing technologies." Yet automation will nonetheless reduce the demand for certain types of labor, forcing societies to adapt.

1. What does the author mean by saying "the evidence for any productivity gains caused by computers is at best ambiguous" (Line 4, Para. 2)?
A
Computers have clearly improved productivity
B
The evidence for productivity gains from computers is inconclusive
C
Productivity gains have been proven to be impossible
D
Only the best evidence supports computer productivity

正确答案：B

解析："at best" 是一个固定表达，意为"充其量，最多"。"ambiguous" 意为"模糊的，不明确的"。因此作者的意思是计算机带来生产力提升的证据最多也只能说是模糊的，即证据不确定、结论性的。

重点词汇：ambiguous (adj.) 模糊不清的；automation (n.) 自动化；displacement (n.) 取代，替代

长难句拆解：Yet the evidence for any productivity gains caused by computers is at best ambiguous.

阅读理解 · 2019年Text3

The relation between formal education and economic development can be studied by comparing educational reform in England with the experience in Germany, which is much more tightly tied to industrial growth. While England lagged behind its rivals in the rate of industrial growth, the proportion of pupils and students aged 5 to 15 had reached 70 percent by the end of the century. Meanwhile, Germany, with a population three times that of England, had in 1870 produced twice as many researchers in science and technology.

2. The author mentions both England and Germany in paragraph 1 to ______.
A
show that England had better education than Germany
B
compare their educational reforms and industrial growth
C
explain why Germany had a larger population
D
prove that education determines economic development

正确答案：B

解析：文章明确指出"The French railway system is the product of a century of central planning"，然后提到"by comparing educational reform in England with the experience in Germany"，这表明作者是在比较两国的教育改革与工业增长的关系。

重点词汇：lag behind 落后于；proportion (n.) 比例；industrial growth 工业增长
