### 1.3 重音规则

#### 单词重音

| 规则 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **双音节词**：通常第一个音节重读 | 'apple, 'student, 'teacher |
| **多音节词**：参考后缀规则 | de'velop, 'window, 'family |
| -tion/-ic/-ical后缀重在前 | na'tion, 'politics, sci'entific |
| -ate结尾多数最后音节重读 | 'calculate, 'celebrate |
| **前缀不影响重音** | re'port, un'fair, dis'able |

#### 句子重音规律

> **实词重读，虚词弱读** — 实词（名词/动词/形容词/副词）通常重读；虚词（介词/冠词/代词/连词）通常弱读。

**例**：The 'book on the 'desk is 'mine.
（介词on、冠词the弱读；book、desk、mine重读）

📌 **重点**：阅读中通过重音判断句子焦点，完形中通过语境判断填词。

## 第二部分：词性（词类）

### 2.1 名词

#### 定义：表示人、事物、地点、概念名称的词。

**分类**：

| 类型 | 示例 | 注意事项 |
|------|------|----------|
| **可数名词** | book, apple, student | 可加a/an或数词，有复数形式 |
| **不可数名词** | water, music, information | 无复数，不能加a/an |
| **集体名词** | family, team, class | 单数形式表复数意义（谓语用复数） |
| **专有名词** | China, John, NASA | 首字母大写 |
| **抽象名词** | love, happiness, hope | 不可数 |

#### 可数名词复数变化规则

| 规则 | 示例 | 特殊情况 |
|------|------|----------|
| **一般加-s** | book→books, dog→dogs | |
| **-s/-x/-ch/-sh结尾加-es** | box→boxes, watch→watches | stomach→stomachs |
| **辅音+y结尾改y为i加-es** | baby→babies, city→cities | |
| **元音+y结尾直接加-s** | day→days, toy→toys | |
| **-f/-fe结尾改f为v加-es** | leaf→leaves, life→lives | roof→roofs, belief→beliefs |
| **辅音+o结尾加-es** | potato→potatoes, tomato→tomatoes | photo→photos, piano→pianos |
| **元音+o结尾加-s** | radio→radios, zoo→zoos | |

⚠️ **不规则复数**（必须死记）：
- man→men, woman→women, tooth→teeth, foot→feet, mouse→mice, goose→geese
- child→children, sheep→sheep, fish→fish, deer→deer
-ox→oxen, cactus→cacti, basis→bases, crisis→crises, analysis→analyses

#### 名词所有格

| 形式 | 构成 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **'s所有格** | 单数名词+'s | the student's book |
| | 复数名词以-s结尾+' | the students' books |
| | 不规则复数+'s | children's toys |
| **of所有格** | A of B（后者修饰前者） | the window of the room |
| **双重所有格** | of + 's/名词性物主代词 | a friend of mine, that son of David's |

### 2.2 冠词

#### 定冠词 the

| 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **特指** | The book on the desk is mine. |
| **唯一** | the sun, the moon, the earth |
| **上文已提及** | I bought a book. The book is interesting. |
| **序数词/最高级前** | The first, the best |
| **姓氏复数形式表一家人** | The Browns |
| **专有名词** | the United States, the Great Wall |
| **乐器** | play the piano/violin/guitar |
| **the+形容词表一类人** | the old, the young, the rich |

#### 不定冠词 a / an

| 区别 | 规则 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **a** | 辅音音素开头 | a book, a university, a European |
| **an** | 元音音素开头 | an apple, an hour, an honest man |

**用法**：
- 首次提及"一个/一本/一位"
- 表示"每一"（each/per）：take the medicine twice a day
- 固定搭配：have a cold, make a decision, take a break, in a hurry

#### 零冠词（不用冠词）

| 场景 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **复数可数名词泛指** | Books are my friends. |
| **不可数名词泛指** | Water is essential. |
| **专有名词** | China, John, Mount Everest |
| **三餐/季节/月份/星期** | have breakfast, in spring, on Monday |
| **语言** | speak English, in Chinese |
| **学科/球类/棋类** | study math, play football, play chess |
| **固定词组** | at night, by bus, go to bed, on foot |

📌 **高频考点**：
- 抽象名词具体化：a success（一件成功的事）, a pleasure（一件乐事）
- 疾病名称：have a fever/cold/cough（但 catch cold, have flu）

### 2.3 代词

#### 人称代词

| 主格 | 宾格 | 所有格 |
|------|------|--------|
| I | me | my/mine |
| you | you | your/yours |
| he | him | his |
| she | her | her/hers |
| it | it | its |
| we | us | our/ours |
| they | them | their/theirs |

**主格/宾格使用规则**：
- **主格**作主语：She is a teacher.
- **宾格**作宾语/表语（口语）：It's me. / Tell him.

#### 物主代词

| 形容词性物主代词 | 名词性物主代词 |
|-----------------|---------------|
| my | mine |
| your | yours |
| his/her/its | his/hers/its |
| our | ours |
| their | theirs |

**用法区别**：
- 形容词性物主代词 + 名词：my book
- 名词性物主代词 = 名词：The book is **mine**.（= my book）

#### 反身代词

| 单数 | 复数 |
|------|------|
| myself | ourselves |
| yourself | yourselves |
| himself/herself/itself | themselves |

**常见搭配**：by oneself（亲自）, for oneself（为自己）, teach oneself, hurt oneself, enjoy oneself

#### 指示代词

| 单数 | 复数 | 远近 |
|------|------|------|
| this | these | 近 |
| that | those | 远 |

⚠️ **易错**：that/those指代前文，避免重复；this/these指代后文或引入话题。
**打电话**：this is...（我是...）/ that is...（你是...）

#### 疑问代词

who/whom/whose/what/which

| 疑问代词 | 用法 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| who | 主语/表语（谁） | Who is coming? |
| whom | 宾语（whom） | Whom did you meet?（口语用who代替）|
| whose | 所有格（谁的） | Whose book is this? |
| what | 什么（未限定范围） | What color do you like? |
| which | 哪个（在限定范围内） | Which book do you prefer? |

#### 关系代词（用于定语从句）

who/whom/whose/which/that/as

#### 不定代词（重点）

| 类型 | 词汇 | 用法要点 |
|------|------|----------|
| **个体** | each, every, all, both, neither, either, one | each/every+单数，both/neither+复数 |
| **数量** | some, any, many, much, few, little, a few, a lot of, several, most | some/any区别（见下方）|
| **全部/无** | all, every, everyone, everything, each, both, all | every=each，不与of短语连用 |
| **全部否定** | none, no one, nobody, nothing, neither, not...any | none of可接of短语 |
| **单数指代** | everyone/everybody, someone/somebody, anyone/anybody, everyone/no one/nobody | 谓语用单数 |
| **复合不定代词** | something, anything, nothing, everything | 形容词置后：something important |

📌 **some与any**：
- some：肯定句/表示邀请或希望肯定回答的疑问句：Would you like some tea?
- any：否定句/疑问句/条件句：I don't have any money. / Do you have any questions?

📌 **few/a few/little/a little**：
- few/little（少，几乎没有）→ 否定含义
- a few/a little（一些）→ 肯定含义
- few+可数，little+不可数：few students, little water

### 2.4 形容词

#### 位置

| 位置 | 规则 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **名词前** | 单个形容词作定语 | a beautiful girl |
| **名词后** | 形容词短语/宾补/表语 | something interesting |
| **定语后置** | enough, present, involved | people brave enough |
| **多个形容词** | 排序口诀：县官行令杀艺西 | 县（限）+官（观）+行（形）+令（龄）+杀（色）+艺（国）+西（材料）|

#### 形容词级

| 级 | 规则 | 示例 |
|----|------|------|
| **原级** | as...as / not as/so...as | as tall as, not so tall as |
| **比较级** | -er / more... | taller, more beautiful |
| **最高级** | -est / most... | the tallest, the most beautiful |

**比较级规则**：

| 规则 | 构成 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 短单词（1-2音节）加-er | +er | taller, smarter |
| 长单词（3+音节）加more | more+原级 | more beautiful |
| 不规则变化 | 记忆 | good→better→best, bad→worse→worst, many/much→more→most, little→less→least, far→farther/further→farthest/furthest |
| 辅音+y结尾 | 改y为i+er | happier, easier |
| 重读闭音节 | 双写最后辅音+er | bigger, thinner |
| 两者比较 | the+比较级 | the more, the better |

**最高级规则**：

| 规则 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 单音节+est | tallest |
| 2音节以y结尾 | happiest |
| 多音节加most | most beautiful |
| 不规则 | best, worst |
| 必须加the | He is the tallest. |

⚠️ **常见错误**：
- ✗ more taller / ✗ most tallest（比较级/最高级不同时使用）
- ✓ elder/older：elder表兄弟姐妹长幼，older表年龄大小

#### 常考形容词后缀

| 后缀 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| -ful | 充满...的 | helpful, beautiful |
| -less | 无...的 | helpless, careless |
| -ous | ...的 | dangerous, famous |
| -ive | ...性的 | active, passive |
| -able/-ible | 可...的 | comfortable, possible |
| -al | ...的 | natural, musical |
| -ly | ...的 | friendly, lovely |

### 2.6 动词

#### 实义动词（及物/不及物）

| 类型 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **及物动词vt.** | 跟宾语 | I love you. |
| **不及物动词vi.** | 不跟宾语 | He runs. |
| **既及物又不及物** | 视语境 | He runs a company.（及物）/ He runs fast.（不及物）|

#### 系动词（连系动词）

| 类型 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **be动词** | is, am, are, was, were |
| **感官动词** | look, sound, smell, taste, feel |
| **变化类** | become, get, turn, go, grow, come, fall |
| **保持类** | keep, stay, remain, stand |
| **表象类** | appear, seem, look |

⚠️ **感官动词后接形容词，不接副词**：
- ✓ The music sounds beautiful.
- ✗ The music sounds beautifully.

#### 助动词

do/does/did/have/has/had/will/would/shall/should/be/been/being/am/is/are/was/were

助动词本身无意义，帮助构成时态/语态/否定/疑问。

#### 情态动词

| 情态动词 | 含义 | 用法要点 |
|----------|------|----------|
| can/could | 能/会 | could更委婉/过去 |
| may/might | 可能/可以 | might更不确定 |
| must | 必须 | mustn't=禁止 |
| have to | 不得不 | have got to = have to |
| should | 应该 | 建议/义务 |
| ought to | 应该 | 比should正式 |
| will/would | 将/愿 | would更委婉 |
| shall | 将/应该 | 用于第一三人称疑问 |
| need | 需要 | 情态/实义动词 |
| dare | 敢 | 情态/实义动词 |
| used to | 过去常常 | 过去习惯 |
| had better | 最好 | had better do |

**所有情态动词后接动词原形（无to的不定式）**。

#### 延续性/非延续性动词

| 类型 | 特点 | 示例 | 能否与时间段连用 |
|------|------|------|-----------------|
| **延续性** | 可持续 | live, study, wait, sleep | ✓ 可用How long...? |
| **非延续性** | 瞬间发生 | die, arrive, start, leave | ✗ 需换成延续形式 |

⚠️ **非延续性动词的延续性替换**：
- die → be dead
- arrive → be here
- start → be on
- leave → be away
- borrow → keep
- buy → have
- join → be in/a member of
- begin → be on

例：He has died. → He has been dead for two days. / He died two days ago.

### 2.8 连词

#### 并列连词

| 分类 | 连词 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| **递进** | and, both...and..., not only...but also... | 和/而且 |
| **选择** | or, either...or..., neither...nor... | 或者/要么...要么... |
| **转折** | but, yet, while, whereas, however, nevertheless | 但是/然而 |
| **因果** | for, so, therefore, hence | 因为/所以 |
| **对比** | while, whereas | 而/却 |

⚠️ **for与because**：
- because从属连词，引原因状语从句：I am late because I missed the bus.
- for并列连词，连接两个并列句，对前者补充说明：I am late, for the traffic was bad.（for不能放句首）

#### 从属连词（见从句部分）

#### 常见连词辨析

| 易混 | 区别 |
|------|------|
| when/while/as | when瞬间/一段时间可；while只接时间段；as表伴随/随着 |
| because/since/as/for | because最强因果；since/As已知原因；for补充说明 |
| although/though/even though | 均可表让步，though最口语，even though最强 |
| whether/if | 宾语从句 whether可句首/of；if不行；条件句只能用if |

|------|------|------|------|
| **主语** | 句子陈述对象 | 句首 | I love English. |

- 感官动词后：see/hear/watch+宾语+动词原形/现在分词
- 命名/认为类：call/name/make/consider+宾语+名词/形容词

## 第四部分：时态

> **16种时态公式速查**：
> 时态 = 时间（现在/过去/将来/过去将来）× 状态（一般/进行/完成/完成进行）

### 4.1 一般现在时

**公式**：主语 + 动词原形/三单 + 其他

| 构成 | 主语 | 谓语形式 |
|------|------|----------|
| 一般情况 | I/you/we/they | 动词原形 |
| 第三人称单数 | he/she/it/名词单数 | 动词+s/es |

**用法**：
1. 习惯性/经常性动作：I drink coffee every morning.
2. 客观真理/事实：The earth moves around the sun.
3. 现在时刻的状态：She is a teacher.
4. 剧本说明/图片描述：The film starts at 8 pm.
5. 条件/时间状语从句表将来：If it rains, I'll stay home. / When he arrives, call me.
6. 替代一般将来时：The train leaves at 9 pm.（按时刻表）

**时间状语**：always, usually, often, sometimes, every day, once a week, in the morning

### 4.2 现在进行时

**公式**：主语 + am/is/are + 动词-ing

**用法**：
1. 正在进行的动作：I am reading a book.
2. 现阶段正在做（不一定是说话瞬间）：I am working on a new project these days.
3. 表将来（确定计划）：I am meeting John tomorrow.
4. 特殊：always/now/constantly+进行时表反复/情感：He is always complaining.

### 4.3 一般过去时

**公式**：主语 + 动词过去式

**用法**：
1. 过去某一时间的动作/状态：I visited Paris last year.
2. 过去习惯性动作（used to/would）：I used to walk to school.
3. 主将从现：If it rained yesterday, I stayed home.（**从句过去时，主句过去将来**）

**时间状语**：yesterday, last week, in 2020, just now, ago, the day before yesterday

### 4.4 过去进行时

**公式**：主语 + was/were + 动词-ing

**用法**：
1. 过去某时刻正在进行的动作：I was sleeping at 11 pm last night.
2. 过去两个动作同时进行（while连接）：While I was reading, he was cooking.
3. 背景描述（用于叙事）：The sun was shining and birds were singing.

### 4.5 现在完成时

**公式**：主语 + have/has + 过去分词

**用法**：
1. 过去发生，对现在有影响：I have finished my homework.（所以现在有空）
2. 过去开始，持续到现在（常与for/since连用）：I have lived here for 10 years.
3. 经历（用ever/never/once）：Have you been to Beijing?

**时间状语**：already, yet, just, recently, before, ever, never, for+时间段, since+时间点, in the past years

⚠️ **易错点**：
- for+时间段 vs since+时间点：for two hours / since 2020 / since two years ago
- 瞬间动词在完成时中不能与for连用，需改为延续性：✗ He has died for 3 years → ✓ He has been dead for 3 years

### 4.6 过去完成时

**公式**：主语 + had + 过去分词

**用法**：
1. 过去的过去（两动作比较，先发生的用过去完成时）：When I arrived, she had left.（我到时她已离开）
2. 过去的过去持续到过去某时：He had lived here for 20 years before he moved.
3. ** hopes/intended/hoped/expected** 表"本打算/未能"：I had hoped to see her.（我本希望见到她）

⚠️ **典型错误**：两动作先后用过去时和过去完成时混淆
- 过去的过去：先发生的 had done，后发生的 did
- No sooner...than, Hardly/Scarcely...when, By the time + 过去时，主句用过完：Hardly had I arrived when it rained.

### 4.7 将来时态

#### 一般将来时

**公式**：主语 + will + 动词原形 / 主语 + am/is/are + going to + 动词原形

| will | going to |
|------|----------|
| 临时决定 | 事先计划 |
| 预测（无依据）| 预测（有依据）|
| 客观将来 | 主观打算 |

**其他表将来**：
- 主语 + am/is/are + to + 动词原形（计划/命令/约定）
- 主语 + am/is/are + about to + 动词原形（即将）
- 现在进行时表将来（已安排）
- 一般现在时表将来（时刻表）

#### 将来进行时

**公式**：主语 + will + be + 动词-ing

**用法**：将来某时刻正在进行的动作：I will be sleeping at 11 pm tomorrow.

#### 将来完成时

**公式**：主语 + will + have + 过去分词

**用法**：将来某时刻前已完成的动作：By 2025, I will have graduated.

#### 将来完成进行时

**公式**：主语 + will + have + been + 动词-ing

**用法**：将来某时刻前持续进行且可能继续的动作：By next month, I will have been working here for 5 years.

### 4.8 过去将来时态

**公式**：主语 + would/should/was going to/was to + 动词原形

**用法**：从过去看将来（多用于从句）：I said I would go. / I thought he was going to leave.

### 4.9 时态对应图

| 时态 | 现在 | 过去 | 将来 | 过去将来 |
|------|------|------|------|----------|
| **一般** | do/does | did | will do | would do |
| **进行** | am/is/are doing | was/were doing | will be doing | would be doing |
| **完成** | have/has done | had done | will have done | would have done |
| **完成进行** | have/has been doing | had been doing | will have been doing | would have been doing |

📌 **高频时态组合**：
- 现在完成时 + for/since → 必考
- 过去完成时（过去的过去）
- 主将从现（条件/时间状语从句）
- 主句将来时 + 从句现在时（If you go → I will wait）

## 第五部分：语态（被动语态）

### 5.1 被动语态构成

**公式**：主语 + be + 过去分词（+ by + 动作执行者）

### 5.2 各时态被动语态

| 时态 | 主动 | 被动 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般现在时 | do/does | am/is/are done |
| 一般过去时 | did | was/were done |
| 一般将来时 | will do | will be done |
| 现在进行时 | am/is/are doing | am/is/are being done |
| 过去进行时 | was/were doing | was/were being done |
| 现在完成时 | have/has done | have/has been done |
| 过去完成时 | had done | had been done |
| 将来完成时 | will have done | will have been done |
| 含情态动词 | can/must do | can/must be done |

### 5.3 被动语态使用场景

1. 动作执行者未知/不重要：The window was broken.
2. 强调动作承受者：The book is loved by millions.
3. 使句子更客观/正式：It is believed that...
4. 信息传递需要（新闻/报告）：The president was shot yesterday.

### 5.4 主动表被动的特殊情况

| 情况 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **need/want/require + doing**（=to be done）| The car needs repairing. (= to be repaired) |
| **be worth doing** | The book is worth reading. |
| **be to blame（该受罚）/ be to let（出租）**| He is to blame. |
| **某些系动词+形容词** | The food tastes nice. |
| **某些不及物动词** | The book sells well. / The door won't open. / The pen writes smoothly. |

### 5.5 It is said/reported/believed/known + that...

| 结构 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| It is said that... | 据说 |
| It is reported that... | 据报道 |
| It is believed that... | 人们相信 |
| It is known that... | 众所周知 |
| It is thought that... | 人们认为 |
| It is suggested that... | 有人建议 |

⚠️ **后接主语从句时，从句用现在时（客观真理/持续事实）或should型虚拟**：
- It is said that he works hard. / It is suggested that he (should) go there.

## 第六部分：虚拟语气

### 6.1 条件句中的虚拟

#### 真实条件句（陈述语气）

| 类型 | 结构 | 时间 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **现在真实** | If + 主+V原形, 主+will/V | 现在 | If it rains, I stay home. |
| **过去真实** | If + 主+过去式, 主+would/V | 过去 | If it rained, I stayed home. |
| **将来真实** | If + 主+were to V/should V, 主+would V | 将来 | If it were to rain, I would stay. |

#### 非真实条件句（虚拟语气）

| 类型 | If从句 | 主句 | 时间 |
|------|--------|------|------|
| **现在虚拟** | If + 主+过去式（be用were）| 主+would/could/might + V原形 | 与现在事实相反 |
| **过去虚拟** | If + 主+had + 过去分词 | 主+would/could/might + have + 过去分词 | 与过去事实相反 |
| **将来虚拟** | If + 主+were to V/should V/were+V-ing | 主+would/could/might + V原形 | 与将来可能相反 |
| **were型虚拟** | If I were you, I would... | — | 与现在事实相反 |

**例**：
- 现在：If I were you, I would take the job.（但我不是你）
- 过去：If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.（但我没努力学习）
- 将来：If it were to rain tomorrow, I would stay home.（如果明天万一下雨）

#### 倒装虚拟（省If）

**公式**：If + 主+were/had/should → Were/Had/Should + 主

**例**：
- Were I you, I would try again. (= If I were you...)
- Had I known it, I would have told you. (= If I had known...)
- Should it rain, I would cancel the trip. (= If it should rain...)

### 6.2 名词性从句中的虚拟

#### 常见动词后的that从句（should型虚拟，should可省）

| 动词分类 | 词汇 |
|----------|------|
| **建议** | suggest, advise, recommend, propose |
| **要求** | demand, require, request, insist, ask |
| **命令** | order, command |
| **主张** | maintain, insist（坚持认为→陈述语气；坚持要求→虚拟）|

**例**：I suggest that he (should) go home. / It is required that everyone (should) be present.

#### 常见名词性从句虚拟

| 句型 | 结构 |
|------|------|
| It is + 形容词 + that + 主语 + (should) V | important, necessary, natural, strange, essential, vital |
| It is + 名词 + that + 主语 + (should) V | a pity, a shame, a surprise, a suggestion |
| It is + 过去分词 + that + 主语 + (should) V | suggested, required, demanded, ordered |

### 6.3 其他句型中的虚拟

| 句型 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **wish + 宾语从句** | 虚拟：过去/现在/将来 | I wish I were rich. / I wish I had studied harder. / I wish I could travel tomorrow. |
| **as if / as though** | 好像（方式）| He talks as if he knew everything. |
| **if only** | 要是...就好了 | If only I had more money! |
| **would rather** | 宁愿 | I would rather you came tomorrow. / I would rather you had come yesterday. |
| **it's (about/high) time that** | 该是...的时候了 | It's time that we left. (= we should leave) |
| **without / but for** | 要不是（含蓄虚拟）| Without your help, I would fail. |
| **lest / in case / for fear that** | 以防（should）| He took an umbrella lest it (should) rain. |

⚠️ **insist辨析**：
- He insisted that he was right.（坚持认为自己是对的 — 陈述语气）
- He insisted that she (should) go.（坚持要求她走 — 虚拟语气）

## 第七部分：非谓语动词

> **非谓语动词**：动词不定式（to do）、动名词（doing）、分词（doing/done）
> **共同点**：都不能单独作谓语，但可担任其他所有句子成分。

### 7.1 动词不定式

#### 形式变化

| 形式 | 主动 | 被动 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般式 | to do | to be done |
| 完成式 | to have done | to have been done |
| 进行式 | to be doing | — |
| 完成进行式 | to have been doing | — |

#### 用法

| 功能 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **作主语** | To learn English is important. / To be loved is a happiness. |
| **作宾语** | I want to go. / I decide to try again. |
| **作表语** | My goal is to become a doctor. |
| **作定语** | I have a lot of homework to do. / the ability to speak |
| **作状语** | I came here to see you.（目的）|
| **作宾补** | I want you to go. / I saw him leave. |

⚠️ **哪些动词后只能接不定式（不能接动名词）**：
- want, hope, expect, wish, desire, intend, plan, decide, agree, promise, refuse, manage, tend, pretend, claim
- would like/love, be willing, be eager, be anxious
- have yet to, be about to, be supposed to

⚠️ **哪些动词后只能接动名词（不能接不定式）**：
- enjoy, finish, suggest, avoid, consider, mind, miss, practice, imagine, risk, escape, deny, admit, postpone, put off
- keep(on), feel like, can't help, give up, put off, look forward to, object to, be used to, get used to, devte...to, stick to, lead to, object to
- it is no use/good + doing

#### to的不定式和介词to区分

**to作介词后接doing**：look forward to, be used to, prefer...to..., in addition to, object to, commit to, devote to, stick to, amount to, respond to

**既可to do也可doing，含义不同**：
- remember to do（记得要做）/ remember doing（记得做过）
- forget to do（忘记要做）/ forget doing（忘记做过）
- stop to do（停下去做）/ stop doing（停止做）
- try to do（努力去做）/ try doing（尝试做）
- like to do（具体喜欢）/ like doing（习惯喜欢）
- prefer to do（更想做）/ prefer doing（更喜欢）
- go on to do（接着做另一件）/ go on doing（继续做同一件）
- mean to do（打算做）/ mean doing（意味着）
- can't help to do（无法帮助做）/ can't help doing（忍不住做）

### 7.2 动名词

#### 形式变化

| 形式 | 主动 | 被动 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般式 | doing | being done |
| 完成式 | having done | having been done |

#### 用法

| 功能 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **作主语** | Learning English is fun. |
| **作宾语** | I enjoy reading. |
| **作表语** | My hobby is painting. |
| **作定语** | a sleeping car, a dining room |

⚠️ **动名词复合结构**：物主代词/名词所有格 + doing
- I appreciate you/your coming.（口语也可用you coming）
- He insisted on me/my going there.

### 7.3 分词

#### 现在分词 vs 过去分词

| | 现在分词（doing）| 过去分词（done）|
|---|---|---|
| **时间** | 主动/进行 | 被动/完成 |
| **语态** | 与主语是主谓关系 | 与主语是动宾关系 |
| **例** | The news is exciting. | The excited students... |

#### 分词作定语

| 类型 | 位置 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 单个分词 | 通常前置 | boiling water, broken glass |
| 分词短语 | 后置 | The boy standing there... |
| 过去分词短语 | 后置 | The book written by him... |

#### 分词作状语

**公式**：分词（短语）+，+ 主句

| 用法 | 时态/语态 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| **作时间状语** | doing（主动/进行）| Hearing the news, she cried. |
| | having done（主动/完成）| Having finished the work, he left. |
| | done（被动/完成）| Given more time, I could do it better. |
| **作原因状语** | doing/done | Being a student, I study hard. |
| **作条件状语** | done（被动）| United, we stand. / Given enough money... |
| **作让步状语** | done | Though tired, he kept working. |
| **作方式/伴随状语** | doing | He sat there, reading a book. |
| **作结果状语** | done | He died, leaving nothing but debts. |

⚠️ **垂悬分词（无依着分词）**：分词作状语时，其逻辑主语必须与主句主语一致。
- ✗ Entering the room, the light was on. → ✓ Entering the room, I found the light on.

#### 独立主格结构

**公式**：名词/代词 + 分词（短语），+ 主句

**作用**：分词有自己的逻辑主语，不受主句主语约束。

| 类型 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **名词+现在分词** | Weather permitting, we'll go outing. |
| **名词+过去分词** | The work done, we went home. |
| **名词+形容词** | He sat there, eyes closed. |
| **名词+介词短语** | He came in, book in hand. |

## 第八部分：定语从句

### 8.1 关系代词

| 关系代词 | 指代 | 在从句中成分 | 可指人/物 |
|----------|------|-------------|-----------|
| who | 人 | 主语/宾语 | ✓人 |
| whom | 人 | 宾语 | ✓人 |
| which | 物 | 主语/宾语/表语 | ✓物 |
| that | 人/物 | 主语/宾语/表语 | ✓人/物 |
| as | 人/物 | 主语/宾语/表语 | ✓人/物 |
| whose | 人/物 | 定语（所有格）| ✓人/物 |

### 8.2 关系副词

| 关系副词 | 含义 | 在从句中成分 | 先行词 |
|---------|------|-------------|--------|
| when | ...的时候 | 时间状语 | time, day, year等 |
| where | ...的地方 | 地点状语 | place, city等 |
| why | ...的原因 | 原因状语 | reason |
| how | ...的方式 | 方式状语 | way |

**关系副词 = 介词 + which**：
- when = at/on/in/during which
- where = at/in/on which
- why = for which
- how = in which

### 8.3 只能用that的情况

| 情况 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **先行词为all/everything/nothing/something/anything/little/much/few** | All that glitters is not gold. |
| **先行词被the only/the very/the last/the same/序数词/最高级修饰** | This is the only book that matters. |
| **先行词既指人又指物** | He talked about the people and the places that he visited. |
| **主句以who/which/what/that开头** | Who is the man that is standing there? |
| **先行词为数词或被数词修饰** | The two students that passed the exam... |
| **先行词为there be句型真正主语** | There is a book that belongs to me. |

### 8.4 只能用which的情况

| 情况 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **非限定性定语从句（逗号隔开）** | My brother, who lives in Beijing, is a teacher. |
| **先行词是that** | That which you said is true. |
| **介词+关系代词** | The person to whom I spoke just now... |
| **从句位于句首（as定语）** | As is known to all, ... |
| **先行词是the way**（可用that/in which）| The way in which he explained it... |

### 8.5 介词提前的定语从句

**公式**：介词 + whom/which + 从句

**例**：
- The person to whom I spoke just now is my teacher.
- The city in which I was born is beautiful.
- The reason for which he was late is unclear.

⚠️ **介词提前后关系代词不能省略**：whom/which不能换that

### 8.6 限定性 vs 非限定性

| | 限定性 | 非限定性 |
|---|---|---|
| **逗号** | 无 | 有（逗号隔开）|
| **意义** | 不可缺少的修饰 | 补充说明 |
| **译法** | ...的... | ..., （who/which...）|
| **关系代词省略** | 作宾语可省 | 不可省 |
| **关系代词** | that, which, who | who, which, whose（不用that）|

### 8.7 the same...as vs the same...that

| 句型 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| the same...as | 同一类（非同一个）| I have the same book as you (do). |
| the same...that | 同一个 | This is the same book that I lost. |

## 第九部分：名词性从句

> **名词性从句**：在句中起名词作用的从句，包括主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句、同位语从句。
> **共同特点**：均用**陈述语序**（主语在前，谓语在后）。

### 9.1 主语从句

**公式**：主语从句 + 谓语 + 其他

**常见引导词**：

| 引导词 | 用法 | 示例 |
|--------|------|------|
| that | 无意义/仅起连接作用 | That he won the prize made us happy. |
| whether | 是否 | Whether he will come is uncertain. |
| what/whatever | 什么/无论什么 | What you need is practice. / Whatever he said is true. |
| who/whoever | 谁 | Who will go there hasn't been decided. |
| which/whichver | 哪个 | Which team will win is not clear. |
| when/where/how/why | 时间/地点/方式/原因 | When we should start is important. |

#### it作形式主语

为避免句子头重脚轻，常用it代替主语从句：

| 句型 | 结构 |
|------|------|
| It is + adj. + that... | It is important that you should study. |
| It is + n. + that... | It is a pity that you missed the show. |
| It is + 过去分词 + that... | It is said that he is a genius. |
| It + vi. + that... | It doesn't matter that you are late. |

📌 **后接主语从句，谓语用should型虚拟**的形容词/名词/过去分词：
- important, necessary, natural, strange, essential, vital, imperative
- a pity, a shame, a surprise, a pity
- suggested, required, demanded, ordered, advised, proposed

### 9.2 宾语从句

**公式**：主句 + 谓语 + 宾语从句

**例**：I believe that he is honest. / Tell me what you want.

⚠️ **宾语从句中的否定转移**：主句谓语为think/believe/suppose/expect/imagines且主语为第一人称时，从句否定转移到主句。
- I don't think he is right.（我认为他不对）
- I don't believe she will come.（我相信她不会来）

#### whether与if的选择

| 用whether | 用if |
|----------|------|
| 宾语从句位于句首 | 一般情况 |
| 与or not连用 | 常规情况 |
| 介词后的宾语从句 | |
| 不定式前 | |
| 主语从句/表语从句 | |

- ✗ I don't know if or not he will come. → ✓ I don't know whether or not he will come.
- ✓ I don't know if he will come.

### 9.3 表语从句

**公式**：主语 + 系动词 + 表语从句

**例**：The problem is whether we can finish it on time. / That is why he failed.

📌 **常见表语从句引导词**：that, whether, as if/as though, because, why, how, what

⚠️ **because与why辨析**：
- This is because...（这是因为...，回答why）
- This is why...（这就是...的原因，结果）

### 9.4 同位语从句

**定义**：对抽象名词的进一步解释说明，与名词处于同等地位。

**公式**：抽象名词 + that + 陈述句

**常跟同位语从句的名词**：
belief（信仰）, fact（事实）, hope（希望）, idea（想法）, doubt（怀疑）, news（消息）, rumor（谣言）, problem（问题）, question（问题）, thought（想法）, order（命令）, suggestion（建议）, proposal（提议）, request（要求）, decision（决定）, discovery（发现）, principle（原则）, promise（承诺）, warning（警告）

**例**：
- The news that our team won made us excited.
- I have no idea that he was the thief.（我没想到他是贼）

⚠️ **同位语从句 vs 定语从句**：
- 同位语从句：that在从句中**不充当成分**（只起连接作用）
- 定语从句：that在从句中**充当成分**（主语/宾语/表语）

## 第十部分：状语从句

### 10.1 时间状语从句

**引导词**：when, while, as, before, after, since, until/till, as soon as, the moment/minute/instant, hardly...when, no sooner...than, once, whenever, every time, by the time

| 引导词 | 用法要点 |
|--------|----------|
| **when** | 强调特定时刻；"正在...时"（=at the time when）|
| **while** | 强调动作持续；只接时间段，不能接短暂动词 |
| **as** | 强调同时伴随；可接短动作 |
| **before** | 在...之前；It wasn't long before...（没多久就...）|
| **after** | 在...之后 |
| **since** | 自从...（主句用完成时）|
| **until/till** | 直到...（肯定谓语用持续性；否定谓语用短暂性）|
| **as soon as** | 一...就... |
| **the moment** | 一...就...（=as soon as）|
| **hardly...when** | 刚...就...（hardly放句首要倒装）|
| **no sooner...than** | 同上 |
| **once** | 一旦 |

⚠️ **when/while/as的区别**：
- while只能接延续性动词：While I was reading, the phone rang.
- when可接短暂或延续性：When I arrived, she was cooking.（短暂到达接进行时）
- as边...边...：As we walked, we talked.

⚠️ **not...until与倒装**：Not until + 时间/从句 + did/had/be + 主语 + 其他

### 10.2 地点状语从句

**引导词**：where, wherever, everywhere, anywhere

**例**：Where there is a will, there is a way. / Wherever you go, I will follow.

### 10.3 原因状语从句

**引导词**：because, since, as, now that, given that, considering that

| 引导词 | 用法 | 位置 |
|--------|------|------|
| **because** | 强因果；回答why；可单独使用 | 主句前/后均可 |
| **since** | 已知原因；"既然" | 主句前 |
| **as** | 浅显原因；说明性 | 主句前 |
| **now that** | "现在既然" | 主句前 |

⚠️ **because与because of**：because后接从句；because of后接名词/代词/动名词
- Because it rained, we stayed home. / Because of the rain, we stayed home.

### 10.4 目的状语从句

**引导词**：so that, in order that, that, lest, in case, for fear that

| 引导词 | 用法 | 特点 |
|--------|------|------|
| **so that / in order that** | 主句+so that+主+can/could/will/would/may/might+原形 | 可省略that |
| **lest / in case / for fear that** | "以防/免得" | 后用should型虚拟（should可省）|

**例**：He studied hard so that he could pass the exam. / Take an umbrella lest it (should) rain.

### 10.5 结果状语从句

**引导词**：so...that, such...that, so that, that（so）

| 句型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **so + adj./adv. + that** | 如此...以至于 | so fast that... |
| **so + many/few/much/little + n. + that** | 数量+that | so many students that... |
| **such + a/an + adj. + 单数n. + that** | such...that | such a good book that... |
| **such + adj. + 复数n./不可数n. + that** | | such good students that... |

⚠️ **so与such的区别**：
- so + 形容词/副词
- such + 名词
- so beautiful → such a beautiful girl（so + adj. + a/an + n. = such + a/an + adj. + n.）

### 10.6 条件状语从句

**引导词**：if, unless, as/so long as, on condition that, provided/providing that, suppose/supposing that, in case, given that

**类型**：

| 类型 | 结构 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| **真实条件句** | if + 现在时, 主句将来时 | 可能发生 |
| **非真实条件句** | if + 过去式/had done, ... | 见虚拟语气部分 |
| **unless** | if...not = unless | 除非 |
| **as long as** | 只要 | 充分条件 |

📌 **主将从现**：If + 现在时, 主句will/may/can/should + 原形
📌 **主句祈使句**：If + 现在时, (please) do...

### 10.7 让步状语从句

**引导词**：although, though, even though/even if, as（倒装让步）, while, whereas, no matter + who/what/when/where/how/which, whoever/whatever/whenever/wherever/whichever/however

**as倒装让步（重点）**：

**公式**：adj./adv./n./v. + as + 主语 + 谓语，主句

**例**：
- Busy as he is, he studies hard.（虽然忙）
- Try as he might, he couldn't open it.（尽管他尝试了）
- Child as he is, he knows a lot.（虽然他只是个孩子）

⚠️ **名词/形容词+as，名词前不加冠词**：Child as he is...（不是A child as he is...）

**although/though vs as**：
- though可倒装可不倒装：Though he is busy / Busy though he is
- as必须倒装
- though可接省略句：He is happy, though.

### 10.8 方式状语从句

**引导词**：as, as if/as though, the way, like（美式）

**例**：He behaves as if he were the owner. / Do it the way I showed you.

### 10.9 比较状语从句

**引导词**：as...as, not as/so...as, than, the more...the more

**例**：He is taller than I (am). / The more you study, the smarter you become.

## 第十一部分：主谓一致

### 11.1 语法一致原则

| 类型 | 谓语形式 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| 单数名词/单个不定式/动名词 | 单数 | To learn is important. |
| every/each + 单数名词 + and + every/each + 单数名词 | 单数 | Every boy and every girl is here. |
| each of.../one of... | 单数 | Each of them is here. |

### 11.2 意义一致原则（谓语视意义而定）

| 类型 | 谓语 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 集体名词表整体 | 单数 | My family is large. |
| 集体名词表成员 | 复数 | My family are all teachers. |
| 山河/地名/书名/学科名 | 单数 | The United States is large. |
| 人民/警察/敌军 | 复数 | The police are searching. |
| 形复名（trousers/glasses/pants/scissors等）| 复数/谓语用复数 | My glasses are broken. |
| a pair/scissors/glasses | 单数 | A pair of scissors is missing. |
| the + 形容词 | 复数（人）| The old are respected. |

### 11.3 邻近一致原则（就近一致）

| 结构 | 谓语与谁一致 | 示例 |
|------|-------------|------|
| either...or... | 邻近名词 | Either you or I am wrong. |
| neither...nor... | 邻近名词 | Neither the teachers nor the principal is there. |
| not only...but also... | 邻近名词 | Not only you but also he is wrong. |
| there be | 邻近名词 | There is a book and some pens. |
| or / nor | 同上 | You or he is to blame. |

### 11.4 特殊名词的主谓一致

| 名词 | 谓语 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| a pair of | 单数 | A pair of shoes is missing. |
| two pairs of | 复数 | Two pairs of shoes are missing. |
| many a + 单数 | 单数 | Many a student has failed.（正式）|
| a number of + 复数 | 复数 | A number of students are present. |
| the number of + 复数 | 单数 | The number of students is 50. |
| half of + n. | 视n.而定 | Half of the apple is eaten. / Half of the students are present. |
| part of + n. | 视n.而定 | 同上 |
|分数/百分数 + of + n. | 视n.而定 | 同上 |
| plenty of + n. | 视n.而定 | Plenty of water is... / Plenty of students are... |
| all of / most of / some of | 视n.而定 | 同上 |

⚠️ **population**："人口"用单数；"人民"用复数：The population of China is large. / Three quarters of the population are farmers.

## 第十二部分：倒装句

### 12.1 部分倒装

> 将助动词/be动词/情态动词提至主语前，实义动词留在原位。

#### 否定词/半否定词提前

| 否定词 | 示例 |
|--------|------|
| not only...but also | Not only did he come, but he was also pleased. |
| never/hardly/seldom | Never have I seen such a thing. |
| rarely/little | Little did I know about it. |
| nowhere | Nowhere else can you find such beauty. |
| no sooner...than | No sooner had I arrived than it rained. |
| hardly...when | Hardly had I sat down when the phone rang. |
| not until | Not until then did I understand. |
| rarely/scarcely...when | Scarcely had I opened the door when... |
| only + 状语（提前）| Only then did I realize the truth. |
| only when/after/before/by... | Only when you work hard will you succeed. |
| Only in this way + 倒装 | Only in this way can you solve it. |
| so + adj./adv. + that | So angry was he that he couldn't speak. |
| so + 主语 + 谓语 | So hard does he work that...（他如此努力以至于...）|
| such + n. + that | Such was his anger that... |

⚠️ **Not only置于句首要倒装**，但Not only位于句中作主语不倒装：
- Not only the students but also the teacher was present.（主语不倒装）

#### other否定词/only类

| 词 | 示例 |
|----|------|
| only + 状语 | Only by working hard can we succeed. |
| so + adj./adv. | So fast did he run that... |
| to such a degree | To such a degree did he work that... |
| here/there + 谓语 | Here comes the bus. |
| then + 谓语 | Then came the police. |

### 12.2 完全倒装

> 将整个谓语移至主语前（主谓完全颠倒）。

| 条件 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| **地点状语 + 谓语（完全倒装）** | Out rushed the children. / Here is your book. |
| **前进/后退副词 + 谓语** | In came the teacher. / Away ran he. |
| **There/Here + be + 主语** | There is a book on the table. |
| **分词/介词短语 + be + 主语** | Sitting there is my teacher. / In the room are some students. |

⚠️ **完全倒装的主语要求**：主语必须是名词（非代词）
- ✓ Here comes the bus. / ✗ Here he comes.

### 12.3 虚拟语气中的倒装

**公式**：If + 主+were/had/should → Were/Had/Should + 主（省If）

| 原倒装前 | 倒装后 |
|----------|--------|
| If I were | Were I |
| If he had been | Had he been |
| If it should rain | Should it rain |

**例**：
- Were I you, I would take this job.
- Had I known it, I would have warned you.
- Should it rain tomorrow, we would cancel the plan.

## 第十三部分：强调句

### 13.1 It is/was...that/who...强调句

**公式**：It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who/whom + 剩余部分

**注意**：
- 强调人：who（主语）/ whom（宾语）
- 强调其他：that
- 可强调任何成分：主语/宾语/状语

**例**（原句：I met him in Beijing yesterday.）：
- It was I that/who met him in Beijing yesterday.（强调主语）
- It was him that/whom I met in Beijing yesterday.（强调宾语）
- It was in Beijing that I met him yesterday.（强调地点状语）
- It was yesterday that I met him in Beijing.（强调时间状语）

⚠️ **强调时间/地点/原因状语只用that，不用when/where/why**

### 13.2 强调句的识别

将"It is/was...that/who..."去掉后，句子成分仍然完整 → 是强调句

**例**：It was because he was ill that he couldn't come.
去掉后：Because he was ill, he couldn't come.（完整 ✓）

### 13.3 强调句与定语从句/状语从句的区别

| 句型 | 辨别方法 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| **强调句** | 去掉It is/was...that剩余部分完整 | It is here that I met him. |
| **定语从句** | that在从句中充当成分 | The place where I met him is here. |
| **状语从句** | 有连词含义 | It was because he was late that...（原因状语从句）|

### 13.4 特殊疑问句的强调

**公式**：疑问词 + is/was it that + 其他？

**例**：
- Where was it that you lost your wallet?
- When was it that you called me?
- Who was it that broke the window?

### 13.5 not...until的强调句

**原句**：He didn't leave until I came.
**强调**：It was not until I came that he left.

## 第十四部分：完形填空技巧

### 14.1 命题规律

1. **首句不设空**：首句通常为完整句子，用于引入主题
2. **实词为主**：动词、名词、形容词、副词占85%以上
3. **上下文线索**：大部分题靠语境而非语法
4. **固定搭配**：介词搭配、动词短语、习惯表达

### 14.2 六大线索类型

| 线索类型 | 识别信号 | 策略 |
|----------|----------|------|
| **原词复现** | 正确选项在原文某处原词重复 | 找到重复词 |
| **同义词复现** | 正确选项是前文词的同义词 | 扩大词汇量 |
| **反义词复现** | 上下文出现反义关系 | 找but/however/yet |
| **逻辑关系** | 因果/转折/递进/解释 | 判断逻辑连词 |
| **固定搭配** | 动介搭配/词组 | 积累词组 |

## 第十六部分：写作必备

### 16.1 书信类作文模板

#### 建议信

**开头**：表明身份+写信目的
I am writing to offer some suggestions regarding...

**正文**：提出具体建议（2-3条）
To begin with, it would be advisable to... Moreover, I suggest that... Finally,...

**结尾**：客套话+期待
I hope these suggestions will be of some help. Your early reply would be highly appreciated.

#### 道歉信

**开头**：表明歉意
I am writing to express my sincere apology for...

**正文**：解释原因+提出弥补方案
The primary reason is that... To make up for the inconvenience, I would like to suggest that...

**结尾**：再次道歉+请求谅解
I sincerely hope you can understand my situation and accept my apology.

#### 感谢信

**开头**：表明感谢
I am writing to express my heartfelt gratitude for...

**正文**：具体感谢事由+对方帮助+对你的影响
Your guidance and support during... not only helped me... but also enabled me to...

**结尾**：再次感谢+期待回报
I will never forget your kindness. Please accept my sincere thanks once again.

#### 申请信

**开头**：表明目的
I am writing to apply for the position of... advertised in...

**正文**：介绍自身优势
I am convinced that I am well qualified for the position. First and foremost, I have gained extensive experience in... Additionally, my communication skills in both written and spoken English are excellent.

**结尾**：期待机会+联系方式
I would welcome an opportunity to discuss my application further. I can be reached at...

#### 投诉信

**开头**：表明身份+投诉事由
I am writing to express my dissatisfaction regarding...

**正文**：描述问题+具体诉求
To begin with, ... Moreover, ... I strongly feel that this matter deserves your immediate attention.

**结尾**：提出具体解决方案
I would appreciate it if you could look into this matter and arrange for a prompt replacement/refund.

### 16.2 图表类作文模板

#### 动态图（趋势变化）

**第一段（描述变化）**：
The chart illustrates the changes in [主题] from [起始年] to [终止年]. According to the chart, [关键数据1] increased/decreased significantly, rising/falling from [数1] to [数2]. Meanwhile, [关键数据2] showed a steady trend, reaching [数3] by the end of the period.

**第二段（分析原因）**：
Several factors account for this trend. First and foremost, ... In addition, ... Furthermore, ... Last but not least, ...

**第三段（总结预测）****：
In conclusion, the statistics indicate that ... It can be predicted that [趋势] will continue in the coming years, mainly due to ...

#### 静态图（占比对比）

**第一段（描述占比）**：
The pie chart illustrates the distribution/proportion of [主题]. According to the chart, [占比最大的部分] accounts for the largest share, taking up approximately [数]%. [占比第二] follows at [数]%, while [占比最小的] constitutes only [数]%.

**第二段（分析原因）**：
The main reasons for this distribution are as follows. ... Moreover, ... In addition, ...

**第三段（总结评论）**：
From the analysis above, we can conclude that ...

### 16.3 议论文框架

#### 对比选择型

**引言**：引出话题
In recent years, there has been a heated debate about whether... Some people believe that..., while others hold that...

**正文**：
On the one hand, those who support A argue that... On the other hand, opponents maintain that... Personally, I prefer A for the following reasons. First and foremost,... Furthermore,... Last but not least,...

**结论**：总结观点
Taking all the factors into consideration, I am convinced that... It is advisable to...

#### 现象分析型

**引言**：描述现象
In recent years/Along with the development of..., it is reported that... has become a common phenomenon.

**正文（原因分析）**：
A variety of factors contribute to this phenomenon. First and foremost,... In addition,... Furthermore,...

**正文（影响分析）**：
The impacts are both positive and negative. On the one hand, ... On the other hand,...

**结论**：建议/总结
In my view, it is imperative that effective measures should be taken to... Only in this way can we...

| **problem / issue / question** | problem待解决问题；issue有争议的问题；question一般疑问 |
| **purpose / goal / aim / target** | purpose意图；goal长期目标；aim短期目标；target具体指标 |
| **family / home / house** | family家人；home家（情感）；house房子（建筑）|
| **people / person / man / human** | people集体；person个体；man男人/人类；human人类（强调人性）|

### 附录D：易混语法点对比

#### too...to / so...that... / enough...to

| 结构 | 含义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| too + adj./adv. + to do | 太...而不能 | I am too tired to walk. |
| so + adj./adv. + that | 如此...以至于 | I am so tired that I can't walk. |
| adj./adv. + enough to do | 足够...可以 | I am tired enough to walk. |

#### whether / if

| 场景 | 用whether不用if |
|------|-----------------|
| 主语从句 | Whether he will come is unknown. |
| 表语从句 | The question is whether we can finish it. |
| 介词后 | I am worried about whether he will come. |
| 与or not连用 | I don't know whether or not he will come. |
| 不定式前 | I haven't decided whether to go. |

#### because / because of

| 区别 | because后接从句，because of后接名词/代词/动名词 |
|------|--------------------------------------------------|

#### in spite of / although

| 区别 | in spite of + n./pron./doing；although + 从句 |
|------|---------------------------------------------|

#### beyond / out of / without

| 词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| beyond | 超出（能力/范围）| beyond my ability, beyond repair |
| out of | 在...之外/出于 | out of danger, out of curiosity |
| without | 没有/不 | without hesitation, without doubt |
